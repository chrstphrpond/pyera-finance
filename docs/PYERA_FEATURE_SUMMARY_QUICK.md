## Market Analysis Summary

### Why These Features?

Based on analysis of leading finance apps:

| App | Key Strengths |
|-----|---------------|
| **YNAB** | Zero-based budgeting, education |
| **Mint** | Automatic categorization, free |
| **PocketGuard** | Simple interface, "In My Pocket" |
| **Monarch** | Comprehensive dashboards, goals |
| **Copilot** | AI insights, Apple ecosystem |

**Pyera's Opportunity:**
- Combine AI power (Gemini) with Android-native experience
- Focus on emerging markets (Philippines/SEA) with multi-currency
- Receipt scanning advantage with ML Kit
- Faster iteration than established competitors
